Welcome to my coffee app called Arabica after the name for the plant species! My website will help you learn about all the different brewing methods.

Espresso

Finely ground coffee pushed through a portafilter at high pressure making a concentrated ounce of coffee.

Pour-over

Medium grind made with hot water poured over the grounds on top of a paper filter.

Siphon

Pour over, but with more science.

French Press

Submerge the grounds and hot water, then squeeze down all the grounds.
